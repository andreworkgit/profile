Rule of Three

About the Project

Rule of Three is an Android application designed to simplify mathematical calculations using the rule of three principle. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who needs to perform quick proportional calculations.

Key Features

  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Quick calculations
  • History of previous calculations
  • Dark mode support
  • No internet connection required

Technologies Used

  • Kotlin
  • Android SDK
  • Material Design
  • Room Database
  • MVVM Architecture
