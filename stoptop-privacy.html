Privacy Policy - Stoptop

Stoptop is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Data Collected

• Vehicle information (name, type, mileage)
• Maintenance history
• Custom maintenance settings
Note: All data is stored locally on your device.

Data Usage

We use your data exclusively for:

  • Managing your vehicle information
  • Calculating maintenance intervals
  • Generating service reminders
  • Improving user experience

Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or share data with external servers.

Sharing

Stoptop does not share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Export your information

App Permissions

Stoptop requires only essential permissions for operation:

  • Storage: to save vehicle data
  • Share: to share the application

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. We recommend checking regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
