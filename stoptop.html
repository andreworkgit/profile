Stoptop

About the Project

Stoptop is a vehicle maintenance management application designed to help drivers keep their vehicles in perfect condition. It provides comprehensive tracking and scheduling features for both cars and motorcycles.

Key Features

  • Track your vehicle's mileage
  • Record and schedule preventive maintenance
  • Customize maintenance intervals
  • Management available for cars and motorcycles

Technologies Used

  • Kotlin
  • Android SDK
  • Material Design
  • Room Database
  • MVVM Architecture
  • WorkManager for notifications
